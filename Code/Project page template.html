Outcome

Mobile banking app to simplify money transfer and withdrawal

Design brief

Problem

Patelco credit union wanted to move into mobile-first digital presence. Our task was to start this process by creating a mobile app focussed on their checking account services.

Context

  • Hypothetical project at General Assembly
  • Problem picked our team to explore applications in community of banking
  • Duration of 2 weeks
  • Aim was to create a checking account experience for the unique case of credit union customers

Team

I worked with William Klemt and Joaille Aruajo, my classmates at General Assembly. My key contributions were in Research, Ideation, Prototyping and Delivery
