Why should you hire me?

" a little bit of slope makes up
for a lot of y-intercept"



This quote is from a lecture by Prof. Ousterhout of Stanford. The argument he makes, is simple: for any decision in life, especially hiring, it is better to bet on a person who has the attitude to learn and grow over any other qualification. I believe I have that attitude.


I am a scrappy, collaborative problem-solver with operational and product management experience able to dive in at multiple levels. Having been an entrepreneur, I have encountered many problems that need me to hustle, read up, talk to as many people as I can, ask dumb questions, ask smart questions, raise my hands to say, “I don’t understand this and I need help”. For every daunting problem I have faced, patience and an open mind proved to be the fuel to get me over the hill, and on the right path.
