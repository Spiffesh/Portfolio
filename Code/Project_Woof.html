Overview

Design brief

Create a mobile app to find trustworthy, reliable & affordable dog-sitters

The problem

  • Dog lovers often need to leave their dog with a dog-sitter
  • Most professional dog-sitters are very expensive
  • Dog-sitters on craiglist etc can't be blindly trusted

The insights

  • One of the key users of the dog-sitting experience is the dog itself
  • It would be important to create a relationship between the dog and the potential dog-sitter
  • Dog-lovers would like going with their dogs on play-dates with dogs of other dog-lovers

Our solutions

  • Create a dog-sitting community
  • Uber Meets Tinder - For dogs
  • Paper prototype made and user flow tested out
